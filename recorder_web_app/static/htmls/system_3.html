Vocal Tone and Style Transfer

Here we are recording differnt Swara Varna sounds of a particular style of a particular speaker. The idea is to only record out the differnt Swaravarna sounds and put them in right way to create voice of same kind (in same Tone and Style).


Procedure For Recording
  1. Select a Voice. Then upload a sample voice of the same voice. You need to take referece of your sample voice each time before giving a take for a particular Swara Varna.
  2. Select a Cropping Amp. Threshold, Sample Analysis Time Gap and Fine Sample Anaysis Time Gap. Put take numbers from 0 to 5 one by one to get all the six Swaravarnas. Pronounce each Swaravarna 3 times as per like you would pronounce that Swaravarna in the name entity with the 'k' Vyanja Varna Sound. For example in a sentence like 'Welcome to our service "kakakak" in our d m i project.' the way you say 'a', 'a', 'a in 'kakakak', you need to say those three 'a's exactly the same way but just putting a 0.5 second gap between each 'a' sound. It is a little bit difficult to understand the way to run this system. Please check out the Explanation Video to get a more proper understanding.
