Word Concatination

Here we will be recording each and every word in a particular Tone and Style , save them all per word, so that we will be concatenating them later to create audio form of any combination of words (sentences).


Procedure For Recording
  1. Choose a particular Voice (A Voice with a perticular Tone and Style i.e a particular style by a particular speaker) and stick to it thorugh out the entire recording process here.
  2. Select a voice. Copy and paste all of your texts in the box (Put all Texts). Click on Analyse. This will just show total number of unique words in the text corpus, number of takes required for the recording and estimated time to record all the takes.
  3. Then Select a Cropping Amp. Threshold and Analysis Time Gap. Put a take number (start with 0) and click on Go. This will show you 8 uniques words. Read all the words in a flow as per the voice first. It's important to keep atleast a 0.5 second gap between each words but preserving the flow while doing recording. Click on Start Recording button to start recording. Keep in mind to use a very good quality microphone with almost 0 noise. After speaking all the words click on Stop Recording button to stop recording.
  4. If everything went find you would get a success message else do a retake. The success message is not enough. You should be listening to all the words by playing them from the poped up play buttons verifying that all the words are properly cropped. If done properly, click on Done to submit the take. Else do a retake.

Vocal Tone and Style Transfer

Here we are recording differnt Swara Varna sounds of a particular style of a particular speaker. The idea is to only record out the differnt Swaravarna sounds and put them in right way to create voice of similar kind.


Procedure For Recording
  1. Choose a particular Voice (A Voice with a perticular Tone and Style i.e a particular style by a particular speaker) with which you would like to match the pronunciation and stick to it thorugh out the entire recording process here.
  2. Select the Voice type from which the voice and tone needed to be transfered. Upload and listen to the Sample audio to build up the flow to pronounce the words and then turn on the mic and pronounce the sample words with a gap within each with a highly noise less microphone. Once the recording process is complete stop the recording.
  3. If all the Swaravarnas are cropped in 3 segments exactly there would be a success message. Then we need to manually see if the 3 Swaravarna chunks are properly done or not by playing the three Swaravarna Sounds. If everything is done properly then click on done else do a retake.
